My Profession Overview

As a software developer, I work at the intersection of computer science and engineering, creating solutions that improve how people interact with web and mobile applications. My role often involves digital design principles, including Photoshop skills to enhance UI/UX elements. My work relies heavily on collaboration with teams focused on design and development to deliver functional and aesthetically pleasing applications.





The IT Technologies

As a mainframe developer, I work with a variety of specialized technologies:

  • COBOL for application development
  • JCL (Job Control Language) for batch job management
  • DB2 for relational database management
  • IMS (Information Management System) for hierarchical database handling
  • RACF (Resource Access Control Facility) for security and access management
  • Version control with tools like Endevor and Git integration

For more details about the mainframe profession and the technologies involved, feel free to explore this reference.
